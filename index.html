About the Science Fair

The Barjo Science Fair is an exciting event for students of Barrington Elementary and Jones Middle School, welcoming all students from kindergarten through 8th grade to participate. It's a hands-on way to get our students engaged in doing science, sparking curiosity and innovation from a young age.

The science fair itself will be held on February 6, 2025, from 6pm to 8pm. However, the journey begins much earlier with a kick-off event on September 17, 2024, at 7:30 pm via the Zoom link provided above. Throughout the year, there will be additional meetings to help guide participants in preparing their projects.

Organized by the Barrington PTO's I Heart Science committee, this science fair not only provides a platform for young scientists to showcase their work but also offers the opportunity for 5th-8th graders to advance to the District 7 Science Fair in March 2025, hosted by Columbus State Community College. There, students can compete for additional prizes and recognition.
