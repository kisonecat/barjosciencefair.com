Project Guidelines & Rules

Most projects are welcome ‚Äî pick a question, test it, analyze results, and present. And if you only want to present at the Barjo Fair, and are not presenting at the district fair, then we can accept essentially any project. However, projects involving humans, vertebrate animals, hazardous materials, or risky procedures require prior approval from our IRB (Institutional Review Board).

If you aim to submit to the regional fair, additional rules and forms will apply (see next section).
