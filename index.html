About the Science Fair

Welcome to the Barjo Science Fair! This community event invites students from Barrington Elementary and Jones Middle School to dive into the joy of scientific discovery. Whether you’re asking “What happens if…?” or testing a creative idea, the Barjo Science Fair is your chance to explore, experiment, and share what you’ve learned.

The fair will be held on February 4, 2026, from 6–8 PM at Barrington Elementary. It all begins with a kickoff webinar on November 5, 2025, at 8 PM (Zoom link above). Throughout the year, we’ll offer meetings and office hours to help students prepare their projects.

Students in grades 5–8 may choose to submit their projects to the District 7 Science Fair on March 21, 2026 at Columbus State Community College, where they can compete for additional prizes and recognition. Younger students are encouraged to participate for fun, creativity, and curiosity!

Project Guidelines

If you are not planning to submit your project to the regional or state fair, your project topic can be about almost anything that interests you! Build, test, or explore — we welcome all curious minds.

However, if your project involves humans, vertebrate animals, hazardous materials, or potentially dangerous procedures, you’ll need pre-approval from our IRB (Institutional Review Board) before beginning. This ensures that all projects are conducted safely and ethically.

For students intending to advance to the regional fair, please be aware that additional rules and forms apply — see the “For 5th–8th Graders” section below for links and templates.
